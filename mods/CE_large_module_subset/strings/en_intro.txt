Welcome to the early access version of Airships: Conquer the Skies.

In this game, you design steampunk vehicles and fight with them in real-time combat. To get started on the basics, visit the Tutorial.

You are also running the Cataclystic expansion pack please take note of the following:
    - money generation can now be increased, build buildings or be build on.
    - enemies too will be able to increase the amount of ships they can maintain/build.
    - you now have new build styles you can try.
    - lag may occur if you take a long time to beat the game and everything is at a standstill.
    - you now have new monster nest, not all are evil but some may be useful in getting new modules.
    - some nest may turn out to be nation destroyers keep a eye out for it.
